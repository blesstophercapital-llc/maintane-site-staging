FAQ

Questions before you grab the kit.

Is this a replacement for professional septic advice?

No. It is a homeowner education kit. If you have sewage backup, septic alarms, standing water over the drain field, severe odor, or repeated slow drains, call a septic professional.

Why is this better than the old checklist?

The old checklist was broad. This kit is organized around the moments homeowners actually search for: smells, slow drains, cost questions, warning signs, bacteria health, and monthly maintenance.

Will the kit push me to buy Maintane?

The kit explains where monthly treatment fits, but it also tells you when a product is not the right next step. That honesty matters for septic care.

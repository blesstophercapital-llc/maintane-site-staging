Keep the trust line bright.

Maintane is for routine monthly septic maintenance. Customers should call a septic professional if they see active signs of a system problem.

  • Sewage backup or surfacing sewage
  • Septic alarm going off
  • Wet or unusually soft drain field
  • Persistent odor after normal use
  • Slow drains across multiple fixtures
  • Unknown pumping history or overdue service

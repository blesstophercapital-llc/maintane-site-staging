FAQ

Questions about natural treatment for smell and slow drains.

Can natural septic treatment fix slow drains?

No treatment should be treated as a drain opener. Slow drains can come from clogs, venting, tank level, or drain-field stress.

Can Maintane help with septic smell?

Maintane supports the bacteria your system depends on as part of monthly tank defense. Strong, sudden, or persistent sewage odor should be inspected.

Should I use harsh drain cleaner in a septic home?

Avoid harsh drain chemicals when possible. They can work against the tank biology septic systems rely on.

When should I call a professional?

Call a professional for backups, strong sewage odor, wet drain-field areas, recurring slow drains, or symptoms across multiple fixtures.

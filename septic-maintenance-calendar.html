Calendar blocks

What to put on the calendar.

Keep the calendar simple enough that you will actually use it.

Monthly

Dose Maintane, check for odor or slow drains, scan the laundry and cleaning routine, and make sure no one is flushing wipes.

Quarterly

Review drain field condition, check for wet or soft areas, inspect visible lids or access points, and update household rules.

After heavy use

After guests, holidays, rental stays, or laundry-heavy weeks, watch for changes in drain speed, smell, bubbling, or outdoor wet spots.

After pumping

Record the date, save the service notes, ask when to pump next, and restart the monthly maintenance habit.

Before buying a home

Ask for septic records, inspection reports, tank location, drain field location, and any history of repairs or backups.

When symptoms appear

Do not bury the signal. If odor, backups, alarms, or wet drain field areas persist, call a professional.

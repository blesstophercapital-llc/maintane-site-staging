The method

How Maintane works

A simple monthly ritual backed by real microbiology. Here's exactly what happens from the moment you subscribe to the moment your septic tank is protected.

1
You choose your bathroom count
Every bathroom in your home has a toilet connected to your septic system. One pod per toilet per month is the right dosage to maintain healthy bacterial levels throughout your tank and drain field. We calculate it for you — just tell us how many bathrooms you have.
2
Your first shipment arrives within 5 days
We ship via USPS or UPS ground. Every package arrives in discreet, recyclable packaging. No subscriptions showing up in branded boxes that shout "septic treatment" to your neighbors.
3
Drop one pod in each toilet and flush
That's the entire protocol. The pod dissolves on contact with water, releasing billions of biosafety-level-1 bacteria. They travel down your drain, through your pipes, and into your septic tank where they begin colonizing within hours.
4
The bacteria go to work
Within 24–72 hours, the bacteria establish colonies and begin breaking down waste, paper, and organic buildup. They survive and reproduce in your tank, maintaining a healthy population between treatments. Monthly dosing keeps the colony strong and your system running efficiently.
5
Your next shipment arrives automatically
30 days later, your next pods arrive. No action required. Your septic system stays maintained year-round. Most customers see measurable improvements — fewer odors, faster drainage, and cleaner tank inspections — within the first 2–3 months.

Subscription & Billing

Subscriptions are billed on a monthly cycle starting on the date of your first order. You will receive an email reminder 3 days before each renewal. You may cancel, pause, or modify your subscription at any time through your account portal with no cancellation fees. Cancellations made after a billing date will take effect on the following cycle.

Shipping

All subscription orders ship free via USPS or UPS Ground. Standard delivery takes 3–7 business days. One-time orders include flat-rate shipping at checkout. We ship within 1–2 business days of each billing date. Expedited shipping is available at checkout for an additional fee.

Returns & Refunds

If you are not satisfied with your first order for any reason, contact us within 30 days and we will issue a full refund. No return required. After the first order, refunds are issued at our discretion for damaged or defective products only. Opened products cannot be returned for hygiene reasons.

Privacy

We collect only the information necessary to process your order and manage your subscription — your name, email address, shipping address, and payment method. We do not sell or share your data with third parties. Payment processing is handled by Stripe and is fully PCI-compliant. We may send transactional and promotional emails; you may unsubscribe from promotional messages at any time.

Product Safety

Maintane contains biosafety-level-1 bacteria and table salt. It is non-toxic and safe for use in homes with children and pets. It is not intended for human consumption. Keep out of reach of young children. In the event of eye contact, rinse with water. The product is not a substitute for routine septic system pumping and inspection.

Contact

For policy questions, billing disputes, or any other concerns, contact us at hello@maintane.com. We respond to all inquiries within 24 business hours.

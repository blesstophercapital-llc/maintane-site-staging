Am I doing this wrong?

Three septic mistakes that feel normal.

Most septic mistakes do not look dramatic at first. They look like everyday household habits nobody questions until the system pushes back.

Mistake 01

Waiting for the house to complain.

Odor, bubbling toilets, soggy grass, and backups are not the beginning of the issue. They are the point where the issue became visible.

Mistake 02

Confusing pumping with maintenance.

Pumping matters, but it is periodic service. Maintane exists for the months between service calls, when the household keeps adding waste.

Mistake 03

Using aggressive cleaners everywhere.

A septic tank is a biological environment. The routine should support that environment, not constantly force it to recover.

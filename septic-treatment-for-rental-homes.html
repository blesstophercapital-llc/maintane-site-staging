FAQ

Questions homeowners ask about rental and airbnb septic care.

Can I use septic treatment in an Airbnb?

Yes, if the system is functioning normally and you follow product directions. Treatment should be part of a larger rental maintenance plan.

What septic rules should rental guests see?

Tell guests not to flush wipes, grease, paper towels, hygiene products, or anything besides toilet paper and waste.

Does treatment replace pumping for rentals?

No. Rentals still need professional pumping and inspection schedules.

Why use a powder for rentals?

Powder dosing is simple to store, explain, and repeat during owner visits or turnovers.

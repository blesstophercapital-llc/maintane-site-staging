# Maintane Footprint Growth System

Use this as the operating system for making Maintane show up in more places online. The goal is not random content volume. The goal is repeated, useful exposure across search, social, referrals, and email capture.

## Weekly Scorecard

Track these every Friday:

- New referring domains earned.
- New citation/profile listings completed.
- Outreach messages sent.
- Replies received.
- Links placed.
- Search Console impressions by asset.
- GA4 sessions by source/medium.
- `lead_form_submit` by source/medium.
- `product_cta_click` by source/medium.
- Shopify `purchase` by source/medium once purchase tracking is flowing.

## Weekly Operating Cadence

### Monday: Pick The Push

Choose one asset and one audience for the week.

Examples:

- Asset: `/printable-septic-rules-for-guests.html`; audience: Airbnb hosts and vacation rental managers.
- Asset: `/septic-inspection-checklist.html`; audience: realtors and home inspectors.
- Asset: `/septic-safe-cleaning-products.html`; audience: natural home and cleaning blogs.
- Asset: `/septic-homeowner-glossary.html`; audience: septic pros and homeowner resource pages.

### Tuesday: Outreach

Send 10 useful, non-spammy outreach messages.

Use this priority order:

1. Resource-page inclusion requests.
2. Expert/source offers for articles.
3. Product listing submissions.
4. Podcast/creator pitch.
5. Directory/citation cleanup.

### Wednesday: Repurpose

Turn the weekly asset into:

- 1 Instagram carousel.
- 1 TikTok/Reel script.
- 1 Pinterest pin idea.
- 1 founder post.
- 1 email snippet.

### Thursday: Internal Support

Add or improve one internal support path:

- Add a contextual backlink from an older blog.
- Add a related resource card to a landing page.
- Improve a page title/meta if Search Console shows weak CTR.
- Add FAQ copy based on a real search query.

### Friday: Measure And Decide

Look at GA4 and Search Console:

- If impressions are growing but CTR is weak, improve titles and meta descriptions.
- If traffic is coming but no clicks/leads, improve CTA placement and bridge copy.
- If leads are coming but no purchases, improve email follow-up and product proof.
- If an asset gets no impressions and no outreach traction after 4-6 weeks, stop pushing it.

## UTM Rules

Use consistent UTM links for anything we control.

Format:

```text
https://getmaintane.com/page.html?utm_source=source_name&utm_medium=medium&utm_campaign=campaign_name
```

Medium standards:

- `referral`: backlink, directory, resource-page listing.
- `social`: organic social profile or post.
- `paid_social`: Meta, TikTok, or other paid social.
- `email`: Klaviyo or manual email.
- `creator`: creator/influencer link.
- `community`: Reddit, Quora, Facebook groups, forums.

Campaign standards:

- `guest_rules_asset`
- `inspection_checklist_asset`
- `smell_checklist_asset`
- `cleaning_products_asset`
- `maintenance_calendar_asset`
- `seasonal_checklist_asset`
- `powder_vs_liquid_asset`
- `seo_citations`

## Outreach Quality Rules

- Lead with the reader benefit, not the product.
- Link to an educational asset first, not the Shopify product page.
- Do not ask for a link in the first sentence.
- Do not exaggerate health, environmental, or repair-prevention claims.
- Keep pitches under 120 words unless sending requested detail.
- Track every pitch in `seo/footprint-tracker.csv`.

## When To Build More Pages

Build new pages only when one of these is true:

- Search Console shows repeated queries we do not serve well.
- Outreach targets ask for a resource we do not have.
- A paid/social hook gets engagement but needs a better landing page.
- A current page ranks but is too broad and needs a cleaner child page.

Otherwise, improve distribution before adding more URLs.

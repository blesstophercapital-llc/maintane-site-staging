## Submission Notes

- Keep the brand name consistent as `Maintane`.
- Use the same website URL everywhere unless the listing is specifically about a blog resource.
- Do not use exaggerated health, environmental, or failure-prevention claims.
- Do not buy bulk backlink packages.
- Do not submit to generic low-quality "add your URL" sites.
- Prefer real profiles, real product listings, review platforms, editorial resource pages, and niche directories with actual human users.

Master Route

The intended visitor path

Every page should make the next step obvious without forcing every visitor to buy immediately. The first yes is the email. The warm escape hatch is Shopify.

1. TrafficGoogle, Meta, IG, TikTok, referral sites

People arrive with a problem, comparison, or trust question.

2. Lane PageSymptom, solution, or trust asset

The page meets the exact search intent and lowers panic.

3. BridgeDosing guide or broad treatment guide

Visitors learn the monthly defense rhythm and honest product limits.

4. First YesWaitlist/email capture

Get the launch link, 10% off, and first-batch availability.

5. Second YesShop Maintane

Warm visitors buy the first jar or return from email/retargeting.

FAQ

Questions families ask before choosing septic treatment.

Is Maintane safe for homes with kids and pets?

Maintane is positioned as kid and pet safe and made without harsh chemicals. As with any household product, store it out of reach and follow the directions.

Can pets play over a septic drain field?

Avoid heavy digging, compaction, or damage over the drain field. If there is standing water, odor, or soft ground, keep pets away and call a professional.

What cleaning products should septic homes avoid?

Limit harsh antibacterial cleaners, bleach-heavy routines, drain chemicals, wipes, and products that can disrupt the bacteria your tank depends on.

How often should a family home use Maintane?

Most homes use one level scoop per toilet, once a month. Larger homes may use more total scoops because dosing is based on toilets.

FAQ

Questions homeowners ask before buying septic treatment.

Can septic treatment help with septic smell?

A natural septic treatment can support the bacteria your septic system depends on, which may help reduce odor related to bacterial imbalance or organic buildup. Persistent or severe sewage smells should be inspected by a septic professional.

Can I use Maintane for slow drains?

Maintane can be used through toilets and drains as part of routine septic maintenance. Slow drains can also come from clogs, plumbing issues, or drain field stress, so severe or worsening slow drains should be diagnosed.

How often do I use Maintane?

Most homes use one level scoop per toilet, once a month. The routine takes less than 10 seconds and requires no measuring beyond the scoop.

Is Maintane safe for homes with kids and pets?

Maintane is designed without harsh chemicals and is positioned for homes with kids and pets. Store it out of reach and follow the directions on the package.

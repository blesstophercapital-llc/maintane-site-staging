Septic Maintenance Calendar

Use this simple calendar to keep monthly Maintane use visible and track the small checks that help you stay ahead between pump-outs and inspections.

Before you start

  • Read the Maintane label and use once monthly as directed.

  • Choose the same reminder day each month.

  • Keep notes if you notice odor, slow drains, gurgling, or yard changes.

  • Call a septic professional for backups, surfacing sewage, alarms, wet drain fields, or unresolved odor.

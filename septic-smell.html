01

02

03

FAQ

Questions homeowners ask about septic smell.

Why does my septic system smell?

Septic smell can come from venting issues, dry drain traps, organic buildup, tank stress, or drain field problems. The source and severity matter.

Can septic treatment help with odor?

A bacteria-based septic treatment can support the biological activity your system depends on, which may improve with odor related to imbalance or buildup. Persistent sewage odor should be inspected.

When should I call a licensed septic pro for smell?

Call a professional if the odor is strong, sudden, persistent, outdoors near the drain field, or paired with slow drains, gurgling, backups, or wet ground.

Simple Septic Defense Guide

A one-page monthly checklist for fewer septic surprises. Use it near your utility area, home binder, or wherever you keep maintenance notes.

Monthly rhythm. Once-monthly use as directed.

Monthly defense rhythm

  • Dose Maintane: once-monthly use as directed.
  • Check bathrooms for odor, bubbling, or slow drains.
  • Look outside for soggy ground or sewage smell near the drain field.
  • Record any pumping, inspection, repair, or alarm notes.
  • Review the house rules with guests, kids, or renters.

Do not flush

  • Wipes, even wipes labeled flushable.
  • Paper towels, tissues, cotton swabs, or dental floss.
  • Feminine hygiene products or diapers.
  • Grease, oils, paint, solvents, or harsh drain chemicals.
  • Medication, cat litter, or anything that belongs in the trash.

Kitchen habits

  • Scrape plates into the trash before rinsing.
  • Let grease cool, then throw it away instead of pouring it down the sink.
  • Use garbage disposals lightly if your home has one.
  • Avoid harsh drain openers as a first response.

Laundry habits

  • Spread laundry across the week when possible.
  • Use normal detergent amounts. More is not better.
  • Avoid heavy bleach or antibacterial product routines.
  • Fix leaks and running toilets quickly.

Warning signs to watch

  • Septic or sewage smell inside the home.
  • Slow drains in more than one fixture.
  • Bubbling or gurgling toilets.
  • Alarm, backup, or wet ground near the drain field.

When to call a pro

Call a licensed septic pro for backups, recurring odors, alarms, sewage surfacing outside, persistent slow drains, or an overdue tank. Maintane supports monthly tank defense; it works alongside pumping, inspections, or repair.

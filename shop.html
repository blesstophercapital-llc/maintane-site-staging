Which pack should I choose?

Choose the single jar if you want to try Maintane first, the 2-pack if you want extra on hand, and the 3-pack if you want the best per-jar value.

How do I use it?

Use one level scoop per toilet, once a month. Add Maintane directly to the toilet and flush. The dosing guide gives the full routine.

Does it replace pumping?

No. Maintane is for simple monthly septic care between professional service visits. It does not replace pumping, inspections, or needed repairs.
